Promoting Environmental Responsibility Through Collective Action

📊 Project Overview

We're proud to launch an innovative recycling program that brings our whole school together in a mission to protect the planet—one plastic cap at a time! Each classroom's mission is to collect plastic caps (chapitas). Although, there's no prize we all share the same passion to achieve the goal of making a real difference. By working together, we’re building a culture of sustainability, responsibility, and environmental care across all grade levels. Let’s show what we can do—cap by cap!
